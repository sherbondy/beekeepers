Get Ready for Bees on Campus!

Starting ~July 3rd

Expect to see honey bees on campus! After much work with EHS and MIT Grounds we now have the approval and support to get hives on campus.

There will be 2 hives behind the student center and 2 hives at Pika.

So now's the time to get involved!


General Safety Tips

Unless in a bee suit, avoid walking along the narrow path (red) and avoid strong perfumes and open drinks (green).

In general, you should be fine. Bees forage for over 1 mile.

We'll have awesome signs posted!

Our Mission

We intend to foster community awareness and understanding of the honeybee (apis mellifera), a remarkable living model of social organization. We adhere to mens et manus: the MIT Beekeeping Club strives for practical, hands-on interaction with honeybees through the upkeep of hives, coupled with intellectual enrichment by viewing bees as a lens for doing science.
